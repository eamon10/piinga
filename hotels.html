  1. InterContinental Alpensia Pyeongchang Resort

     The InterContinental Alpensia Pyeongchang Resort is an Alpine village nestled within the beauty of the Taebaek Mountains. The resort is rated 4.4/5 stars and is our personal favourite place to stay in PyeongChang. They offer many leisure activities within their hotel including

    • Two Olympic class ski slopes
    • Three scenic golf courses
    • Ocean 700 - Koreas most modern indoor water park
    • Spa - Offering hydro therapy, sauna room, and steam bathing
    • The Oxy Lounge - a beautiful fireplace lounge with floor to roof windows offering an amazing view.
    • Children's Club - drop off the kids at the children's club where they can play allowing you time to relax


  2. Yongpyong Resort Tower Condominium

     Yongpyong Resort is located 700 meters above sea level about 200km from Seoul, South Korea's capital city. With an annual snowfall average of 250cm, the resort has a beautiful snowy environment, allowing a wide array of winter sports including skiing from mid-November to early April. On its 4,300 acres, you will find a 45-hole Golf course,28 ski slopes, European-style condominiums and many other leisure facilities that can be enjoyed by the whole family.

    • Gondola tour of Balwangsan Mountain - Offering hydro therapy, sauna room, and steam bathing
    • Tobogganing - Families with children can enjoy the 200 meter wide tobogganing hill.
    • Mountain Coaster - Ride down the 1,300 meter long steep slope with sharp curves at 40km/h.
    • Zip line
    • Golf - Three separate golf courses to chose from
    • Peak Island water park - Indoor, all year water park
